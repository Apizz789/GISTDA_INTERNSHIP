[SIRACE]

(The Simulation website for the risk area when the parts of rocket crashes to Earth)




What Is SIRACE

    SIRACE is short for The Simulation website for the risk area when the parts of rocket crashes to Earth.

   SIRACE has developed and designed with the idea from Air Hazard Prevention and Alarm System To simulate the fall back to Earth of a piece of material from a rocket. In the SIRACE , it has been designed to be able to use easily and see the picture through an 3D animation format mainly using Cesium Js on Web browser. In SIRACE there is a rocket launch station that references real world locations. And we need information about the fall coordinates of the part rocket from you. That's all , then can the cool model be successfully created.



How To Use

SIRACE it's simply and easy to use.
1. You MUST to choose a rocket launch station.
2. You MUST enter your geographic coordinates, in our designated formats such as latitude-longitude, MGRS and UTM coordinate.
